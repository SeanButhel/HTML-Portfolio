The Best Movies According to Sean

My top 3 Movies of All-Time.

Transformers

This is the first movie that popped into my head. Megan Fox and Alive Cars I guess.

Beserk

Very gruesome yet realistic animation on what real life war and fighting would look like. Unlike most PG censored unrealistic shows out there.

Sword Art Online

One of my favourite animes that really got me hooked. If you like games and ever wanted an idea of what it would look like to be trapped in a virtual game this is for you!
